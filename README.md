<h1 align="center">Incredible-INDIA</h1>
<p>
  The **Incredible India website** is the official tourism portal of the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India.
  It serves as a digital gateway to explore the country’s rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and vibrant traditions.
  The site provides detailed information about destinations across all regions of India, including iconic landmarks such as the
  Taj Mahal, Jaipur palaces, Kerala backwaters, Goa beaches, the Himalayas, and bustling cities like Mumbai.
  <br>
  Alongside destination guides, the website features multimedia galleries,
  travel itineraries, and updates on festivals, events, and tourism fairs. Designed to be accessible and user‑friendly,
  it offers multilingual support and a 24x7 tourist helpline, ensuring that both domestic and international visitors
  can discover and experience the beauty and diversity of India.

</p>
