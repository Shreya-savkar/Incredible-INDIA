Destinations

For every bucket list

Pick from iconic cities, peaceful hill stations, heritage towns and more.

Delhi

Capital of India. Mughal forts, bustling bazaars, street food and monuments like India Gate and Qutub Minar.

Udaipur

The Lake City: palaces, forts, vibrant markets and royal Rajasthani culture.

Banaras

Palm-fringed canals, houseboats, ayurveda retreats and tranquil villages.

Srinagar

High-altitude deserts, monasteries and some of the most dramatic roads on earth.

Experiences

Choose your way to explore

Whether you want adrenaline, peace, spirituality or culture – India has it all.

Adventure

Trek in the Himalayas, river raft in Rishikesh, scuba dive in the Andamans or try desert safaris in Rajasthan.

Nature & Wildlife

Spot tigers in national parks, relax in hill stations, and wander through lush forests and tea estates.

Heritage & Culture

Explore forts, palaces, museums, art, music, dance and festivals that have evolved over thousands of years.

Spiritual Journeys

Visit ancient temples, ghats, monasteries and pilgrimage towns across the country.
